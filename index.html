Translation

Translating is a way to connect you with the world, and whether you require a translation for your business or for personal reasons, my translations help open you up to new possibilities. I specialise in the following areas:

  • Legal - such as rulings and judgments, contracts, agreements, police reports, court decisions and legal aid documents.
  • Medical - covering topics such as cardiology, obstetrics and gynaecology, medical diagnostics, autopsy reports, hospital discharge summaries, patient information brochures and others.
  • Literary – including a variety of subjects such as academic papers, palaeontology, poetry, editorials and journalism, articles about the European Union, and other assorted topics.
  • General - such as CVs, diplomas and certificates (birth, school, marriage).
    • Although I specialise in the aforementioned fields, I also translate documents pertaining to politics, government, business and marketing.

    Proofreading

    We all make mistakes from time to time…

    At times, it can be difficult for translators to take a step back from a translation because so much effort has been placed on rendering it into the target language.

    Hiring an independent proofreader may be useful in providing you or your client with a more natural sounding translation and to make sure everything in the original document is contained in the translation!

    Proofreading involves comparing the translation to the original Polish text and looking for inconsistencies with grammar, terminology, meaning, consistency, and overall fluidity that may have been overlooked.

    Editing

    I also edit English-language texts:
    • Academic Essays
    • Theses
    • Dissertations
    • Websites
    I not only focus on grammar, punctuation, terminology and consistency, but pay special attention to the cohesion of the text. Do ideas flow logically from one to the next? Do arguments make sense? Is there a balance between your use of primary and secondary sources? If you think not, please do not hesitate to contact me for your editing needs!
